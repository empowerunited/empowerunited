Process

We only use last generation versions to most efficiently deal with the technical debt in order to ship high quality products now and in the future.

  • Scoping

    Together we define the scope of the project; we will fix the first iteration as precisely as possible and sketch the following iterations in continuous delivery dynamics.

  • Contract

    Together we negotiate the terms of our relationship, clarify all details and sign a contract.

  • Interaction

    Еach iteration starts with a very precisely defined feature set and UI.

  • Continuous Delivery

    EUS implements the solution using scrum based continuous delivery dynamics.

  • Customer Review

    We always appreciate your feedback on the development so we have fully automated the deployment process. Аfter every delivery, you review the solution so far, give your feedback and it is incorporated in the next delivery.

  • Deployment

    Our CI server is performing automated deploys on the staging environment, where you can always check the current status. Once the full scope of the contract is met, well tested, and all your feedback is reflected, we deploy the solution on the production environment and it is then open for public use.

  • Maintenance

    Once the solution is deployed we still keep an eye out for fixing critical and important issues and maintaining the site as necessarry.

Empower on Rails

In 2011 Empower United Services (in partnership with Starbucks) created series of events, dedicated on introducing the, still new for Bulgaria back then, Ruby on Rails framework. Initially, we started with our team members leading the conferences, but soon after that decided it worths spreading the word and having other leaders of the community as speakers also. In three consecutive conferences, we brought our speakers’ expertise to almost 300 Bulgarian IT specialists and successfully covered most of the basic RoR topics.

  • Client: Empower United
  • Date: January 2011
  • Service: Web Development

Zadrujno

Zadrujno.bg was the first Bulgarian daily deal website. In 2012 Zadrujno wanted to rewrite some of its existing .NET technology and introduce various additional features, which were required by the market and its clients. In addition to that, the business owners needed to automatise some of their operations in order to fully concentrate on the business development. For almost six months EUS worked on this technology and built from a scratch the entire front end, back end, administration as well as partially – the design of Zadrujno’s site. We built and customized the administration panel, which was supporting the background processes. We also introduced various checkout solutions with different payment methods. As a result, the entire user experience for Zadrujno’s clients was improved and helped the business owners define more specifically Zadrujno’s market needs and future business model development.

  • Client: Motivi Tours
  • Date: May 2012
  • Service: Web Development

Motivi Tours

Motivi Tours is a boutique licensed tour operator, based in Sofia, which specialises in offering customized travel solutions for its clients. Motivi needed a technical solution, which would allow their clients to browse through Motivi’s offers and after a simple, yet comprehensive registration, to checkout using a preferred payment method, with the selected product. Motivi also wanted a solution which to the fullest extent possible would simplify the offline element of the entire purchase. For less than a month, EUS managed to implement this solution and launched it in production for all Motivi’s clients.

  • Client: Motivi Tours
  • Date: July 2011
  • Service: Web Development

Yatoto Beta

Yatoto is a social network for volunteerism and charitable giving that connects people who want to do good. Yatoto has over 100,000 users in Bulgaria and has distributed over 500,000 leva back to thousands of people and tens of charitable organizations. Yatoto’s unique innovation is called Renewable Advertising TM, which guarantees attention to advertising content while allocating advertising revenue back to consumers.

  • Client: Yatoto
  • Date: November 2013
  • Service: Web Development

Club 50 Plus

Club 50 Plus is an online social enterprise, which helps Bulgarian seniors live happier and more productive lives. After more than two years on the market, Club 50 Plus needed a comprehensive technological solution to its new business model, aiming to directly empower mature people in Bulgaria, who consist of more than 40% of the total population of the country. EUS addressed this need by implementing an entirely new RoR technical infrastructure, which allows tens of thousands of people to participate in user-generated content competition dynamics and brings real value to client’s corporate partners.

  • Client: Club 50 Plus
  • Date: January 2013
  • Service: Web Development
